How It Works

Three steps to total recall.

01 —
Ambient Recording
Onvoice runs silently on your phone or laptop. It captures conversations, meetings, voice notes, and ambient audio — all locally, all private.
02 —
AI Contextualisation
Every recording is processed by AI that understands who said what, what decisions were made, what needs follow-up — and writes it all in fluent prose.
03 —
Perfect Memory
Search, revisit, or ask AI questions about anything you've ever said or heard. Your memory, finally externalised and always available.

Generated Notes

Capabilities

Built for the way you actually live.

🎙
Always-On Capture
Runs as a background process with minimal battery impact. Captures only when audio is detected — not silence.
🔒
On-Device Processing
Raw audio never leaves your device. Transcription happens locally. Only structured notes are sent to the cloud — encrypted.
🧠
Contextual AI Notes
Not just transcripts. The AI identifies decisions, action items, sentiment shifts, open questions, and key names — automatically.
🔍
Semantic Search
Ask "What did Sarah say about the SDK?" and get the exact moment, in context, instantly — even from three months ago.
🔗
App Integrations
Push notes to Notion, Obsidian, Linear, Slack, or your calendar. One note, everywhere you already work.
📅
Daily Digest
Every evening, a clean summary of your day lands in your inbox — everything you heard, distilled into two minutes of reading.
